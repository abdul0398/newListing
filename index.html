    The latest addition to the series of residential developments along Lentor Gardens is Lentor Mansion, a 99-year leasehold new launch condo with 533 units, built on an expansive land size of over 21,000 square metres. Within walking distance to Lentor MRT station, residents can commute to the city centre directly via the Thomson-East Coast Line (TEL). Those who drive will also appreciate its proximity to the Seletar Expressway (SLE) and Central Expressway (CTE) for island-wide connection.
